# Product Requirements Document: Prism Commodities Website

## 1. Executive Summary

This Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlines the specifications for a new, premium website for Prism Commodities. The objective is to create a unified online presence that effectively showcases the company's expanded service offerings, encompassing both its established agricultural commodities business and newly integrated petroleum and energy services. The website will be designed to appeal to a global B2B audience, emphasizing professionalism, reliability, and a comprehensive supply chain network. It will integrate key information and service descriptions from both the existing Prism Commodities website [1] and the Jupiter Petroleum services page [2], ensuring a cohesive brand experience.

## 2. Project Goals and Objectives

The primary goal of this project is to develop a modern, user-friendly, and visually appealing website that:

*   **Consolidates Service Offerings**: Seamlessly integrates agricultural commodities and petroleum/energy services under the Prism Commodities brand.
*   **Enhances Brand Image**: Establishes Prism Commodities as a leading global player in both agricultural and energy sectors, reflecting a premium and trustworthy image.
*   **Improves User Experience**: Provides intuitive navigation and clear information architecture for diverse user needs.
*   **Facilitates Lead Generation**: Incorporates clear calls-to-action and contact mechanisms to encourage inquiries and partnerships.
*   **Showcases Global Reach**: Visually represents the company's extensive global footprint and operational capabilities.

## 3. Target Audience

The website's primary target audience includes:

*   **International Buyers and Traders**: Seeking reliable suppliers for agricultural commodities and petroleum products.
*   **Logistics and Supply Chain Partners**: Interested in collaboration for efficient global distribution.
*   **Producers and Farmers**: Looking for origination and market access for their agricultural products.
*   **Industry Stakeholders**: Including investors, regulatory bodies, and potential employees.

## 4. Brand Identity and Design Guidelines

### 4.1. Logo and Visual Elements

The existing Prism Commodities logo, featuring a prism/triangle motif with purple, blue, and green gradients [3], will be central to the website's visual identity. This logo effectively conveys the company's diverse yet integrated operations and global perspective.

## 6. Key Features and Functionality

*   **Responsive Design**: The website will be fully responsive, ensuring optimal viewing and interaction across all devices (desktops, tablets, and mobile phones).
*   **Premium Visuals**: High-resolution imagery and professional photography will be used throughout the site, depicting logistics, agricultural processes, and energy infrastructure.
*   **Clear Calls-to-Action (CTAs)**: Strategically placed CTAs to guide users towards inquiries, partnerships, or further information.
*   **SEO Optimization**: The website will be built with SEO best practices in mind to ensure high visibility in search engine results.
*   **Security**: Implementation of SSL certificates and other security measures to protect user data and ensure a secure browsing experience.

## 7. Technical Requirements

*   **Platform**: A robust and scalable content management system (CMS) will be utilized (e.g., WordPress, Webflow, or a custom solution).
*   **Hosting**: Reliable and secure hosting with high uptime.
*   **Analytics**: Integration with web analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to track website performance and user behavior.
*   **Browser Compatibility**: Full compatibility with modern web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

## 8. Future Considerations

*   **Multilingual Support**: Potential for adding multiple language options to cater to a diverse global audience.
*   **Client Portal**: A secure portal for existing clients to access specific information or manage their accounts.
*   **News/Blog Section**: To share industry insights, company updates, and thought leadership content.
